What I do

Web & Mobile Apps

With more than 5 years of web experience in different roles, from online marketing to full-stack web development, I now focus on the rapid development of mobile and web apps using web technology for new product development.

New Products & Startups

Creating new products takes more than technical know-how. It takes an integrated, iterative, and customer-centric approach. Combining agile development with lean startup practices.
My technical and business background and my startup experience (working with and in startups) put me in an ideal position to face the challenge of developing new products and helping my clients create successful products.

The Technology

When developing new products, the technology needs to reflect the special needs for the fast pace and iterative development. That's why I develop mobile apps as hybrid apps, that is web technology based development, converted into mobile apps. For this I use Ionic Framework, Angular.js, and Apache Cordova. For server-side services I use Node.js or Python, depending on the requirements of the project.

About Me

I have a diverse background in software and business, with master (or equivalent) degrees in both, practical computer science and business administration. I see myself as a jack-of-all-trades, master of some. I have worked in the web domain since 2011 – starting with online marketing and adding web development in 2014. My real passion and where I see my skills come together is in creating and helping others create great products. I have always had an entrepreneurial streak. Tried, failed with, and learned from several startup experiences. And in a startup it's always about creating a product that your users love.
